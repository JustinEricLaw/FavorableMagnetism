About Me

I’m an Interface Designer and Developer based in Seattle, Washington. I’m passionate about working across the product spectrum to create intuitive and efficient user experiences. I love making products come to life, whether it involves sketching wireframes, designing interfaces, building prototypes, or writing front-end code. I've had the privilege to work with some fantastic clients over the years, including Urbanimmersive, Tourbuzz, Georgia Tech, Tere O’Connor Dance, and Pacific Northwest Ballet.
