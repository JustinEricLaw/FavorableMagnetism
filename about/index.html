About Me

I like to build things. I've been working as a designer for over a decade, focusing on interface design and development for the past five years or so. Whether it involves sketching wireframes, designing interface mockups, building prototypes, or writing front-end code, I enjoy the process of making products come to life. I'm especially interested in creating experiences that are intuitive and efficient. I've had the privilege to work with some fantastic companies and clients over the years, including Urbanimmersive, Tourbuzz, Georgia Tech, Tere O’Connor Dance, Pacific Northwest Ballet, and Dance Theater Workshop. In my spare time I can usually be found creating a synthesizer patch, programming a drum machine, listening to records, or hiking in the beautiful Pacific Northwest.
