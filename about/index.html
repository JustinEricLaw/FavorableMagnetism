About Me

I've been working as a designer for over a decade, focusing on interface design and development for the past six years. Whether I'm sketching wireframes, designing mockups, building prototypes, or shipping code, I enjoy the process of making products come to life. I've had the privilege to work with some fantastic clients over the years, including Urbanimmersive, Tourbuzz, Georgia Tech, Tere O’Connor Dance, and Pacific Northwest Ballet.

Interests

In addition to my love of design, I also enjoy writing and recording music, synthesizers, drum machines, college football, premier league soccer, open world gaming, hiking in the Pacific Northwest, gardening, and listening to music.
