About the Project

Avid is an industry-leading technology and multimedia company. They are widely known for their popular audio and video editing software solutions which are used in music and movie studios around the world. I began working with Avid in 2022, and shortly after, we began the process of redesigning and rebuilding Avid's website, which required an entirely new shopping cart and checkout process.

My Contribution

I provided lead interface design on Avid's new checkout process, collaborating with key stakeholders throughout the company to cover a complex array of product offerings and options. My process included interface design, UX design, and the creation of new design system components. I also provided technical writing to guide the web development teams, ensuring that interaction, accessibility, and responsive layouts were built according to specifications.
