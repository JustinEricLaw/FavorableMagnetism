Tere O’Connor Dance

Website Redesign

Responsive website for Tere O’Connor Dance, a modern dance company based in New York City. I provided all design and development duties for this project, which included content strategy, mockups, and building a custom Wordpress theme. The site highlights upcoming performances, media, company information, and various teaching resources. I also customized the Wordpress admin area to make site updates easier for the company.
