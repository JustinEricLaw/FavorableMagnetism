About Tere O’Connor Dance

Tere O’Connor Dance is a modern dance company based in New York City. The company has created over 40 original works and has presented them throughout the US, Europe, South America, and Canada. O’Connor is also an educator, having taught at festivals and universities around the globe for over 25 years.

My Contribution

I have worked with Tere O’Connor Dance on various design projects over the years. In 2014, I was asked to redesign his website into something more modern, which would also be easier to update. I provided all design and development duties for this project, which included content strategy, mockups, and a custom Wordpress theme. The responsive site highlights upcoming performances, company information, media, and teaching resources. I also customized the Wordpress admin area to make site updates easier for the company.
