About

Favorable Magnetism is the online home of Justin Law. I am a digital designer based in Atlanta, Georgia, with a passion for interface design, responsive web design, branding and creative direction. I am currently Senior Graphic Designer at Georgia Tech’s College of Computing. Some of my past clients include Pacific Northwest Ballet, Dance Theater Workshop, Hunter College and Tere O'Connor Dance. I grew up in Live Oak, Florida, and hold a Bachelor's Degree in Visual Arts from Florida State University.
