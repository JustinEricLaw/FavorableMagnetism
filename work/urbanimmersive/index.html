What are Single Property Websites?

Urbanimmersive is a leading SaaS business solution for real estate photographers. One of their offerings is known as a "Single Property Website", which is a highly customizable landing page for agents to highlight a property. When a photographer is hired by an agent to capture media assets of a property, they often include a Single Property Website along with the media, offering agents another tool to showcase their listings. These property websites offer a variety of design templates, with full customization of colors and fonts, as well as template options and feature settings to further refine the overall presentation.

My Contribution

I provided interface design and development on a robust family of over 20 highly customizable design templates, viewed by millions of potential home-buyers. I also provided UI/UX design on numerous features throughout the platform, working with the customer support team to gather feedback, eliminate pain points, and provide flexible solutions for customers around the world.
