Tourbuzz

UI/UX Design & Development

Redesign of customer client panel for Tourbuzz, a software company providing tools for real estate photographers. I provided lead interface and experience design on this initiative, with a focus on creating an intuitive and responsive interface for all modern devices. I worked with the company's customer support team to locate and resolve numerous pain points from the previous version. I also provided front-end development assistance with general styling and responsive CSS.
